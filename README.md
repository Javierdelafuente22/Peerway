# AI-Driven P2P Energy Trading App

## The Problem
Historically, energy flowed in one direction: from massive power plants down to passive consumers. Today, people have solar panels, batteries, and microgrids which allow them to buy and sell electricity with their neighbours. While **Peer-to-Peer (P2P) energy trading** offers a vital solution to rising grid costs, volatility, and transmission losses, current platforms fail at the human level. They are too complex, opaque, and time-consuming, creating a high barrier to entry for everyday consumers.

## The Solution
A **human-centered, AI-driven P2P energy trading application**. By automating complex market interactions and introducing a conversational interface, the platform empowers non-experts to unlock financial and environmental savings, passing renewable investment returns to the consumer while de-risking the transition for governments.

## The Architecture (User Flow)
* **Data Ingestion:** Seamless integration with smart meter data covering consumption patterns and distributed assets (solar, EV, battery).
* **Algorithmic Trading:** An AI agent automatically generates optimized bids and asks based on net demand and real-time market factors.
* **Market Execution:** The agent executes trades within a local P2P microgrid (10 agents + main grid) to secure the best spread.
* **Impact Visualization:** A transparent dashboard translates complex market data into highly readable financial and carbon savings.
* **Conversational Control:** A natural language chatbot (LLM) allows users to adapt their trading strategy instantly based on lifestyle shifts (e.g., "I'm going on vacation," or "I'll be working from home").
